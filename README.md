### 🛸 About My Work
I am passionate about **Autonomous Robotics** and **Computer Vision**. Currently, I'm focused on developing high-performance indoor drones that can navigate complex environments without GPS.

- 🔭 **Current Focus:** Building an autonomous indoor drone powered by **Raspberry Pi 5**.
- 🧠 **Intelligence:** Implementing **SLAM** and 3D mapping using **RTAB-Map**.
- 👁️ **Vision:** Leveraging **OAK-D** spatial AI cameras for depth perception and obstacle avoidance.
- 🌱 **Learning:** Deep-diving into **C** for low-level optimization and mastering **ROS2** architecture.
